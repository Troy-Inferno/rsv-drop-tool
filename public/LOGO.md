# Logo

Drop your logo file here as **`RSV-Drop-Tool-Logo.png`** (this exact name).

It is used by:

- The page header (`src/app/page.tsx` — `<Image src="/RSV-Drop-Tool-Logo.png" />`)
- The favicon (`src/app/icon.tsx`)
- The Apple touch icon (`src/app/apple-icon.tsx`)
- The Open Graph share image (`src/app/opengraph-image.tsx`)
- The Twitter share image (`src/app/twitter-image.tsx`)

If the file is missing, all of the above fall back gracefully without crashing the build.
